# What to take with you on the trail

We go to the trail **well-shoeed**, preferably in high mountain boots.
We take with us **a backpack**, which can be converted into a stool if possible.
The proverb says: *"Better to sit badly than to stand well."*

To protect ourselves from various weather conditions, we take:

- **a hat** for strong sun,

- **an umbrella** or a light raincoat.
